### Business Domain :

- Human Resources & Workforce Analytics
    - The focus is on understanding employee behavior, satisfaction, and retention to optimize organizational
      performance and reduce operational costs associated with hiring and training.

### The Business Challenge

a. Specific Business Question or Challenge

- What are the primary drivers of voluntary employee attrition at IBM, and which specific employee segments are most at
  risk of leaving?

  - High employee turnover is a critical problem for any organization because it leads to lost productivity,
  increased recruitment costs, and a loss of institutional knowledge.
